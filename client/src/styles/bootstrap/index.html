What is climate change?

A term given to the phenomenon of rising temperatures and a shift in weather patterns worldwide, believed to be caused by human activities.

Chiras, Daniel D. "Global Climate Change." Encyclopedia Americana. Scholastic Grolier Online. Accessed 27 Mar. 2017.

What are fossil fuels?

Forms of fuel that are formed from the remains of living things that died millions of years ago. Common forms include oil, natural gas, and coal.

"Fossil Fuels." The New Book of Knowledge. Scholastic Grolier Online. Accessed 27 Mar. 2017.

How do fossil fuels relate to climate change?

The combustion of fossil fuels release greenhouse gases, which trap the heat in the atmosphere from escaping into space. The most notable byproduct is carbon dioxide.

"Fossil Fuels." The New Book of Knowledge. Scholastic Grolier Online. Accessed 27 Mar. 2017. Chiras, Daniel D. "Global Climate Change." Encyclopedia Americana. Scholastic Grolier Online. Accessed 27 Mar. 2017.
