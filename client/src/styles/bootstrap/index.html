Knowledge and information on avoiding fossil fuels

What is climate change?

A term given to the phenomenon of rising temperatures and a shift in weather patterns worldwide, believed to be caused by human activities.

Another common name for climate change is global warming.

Climate change is mainly caused by the greenhouse effect, which describes the trapping by the earth's atmosphere of heat energy radiated from the sun.

The greenhouse effect is triggered by greenhouse gases, which prevent heat from escaping the atmosphere.

Greenhouse gases are the byproduct of burning fossil fuels.
