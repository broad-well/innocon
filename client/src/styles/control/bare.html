nouvEnergy Home Browse Products Usage Calculator About This Welcome to nouvEnergy, an application that spreads awareness of reducing fossil fuel reliance. What are Fossil Fuels? Substances that formed underground millions of years ago from dead plants and animals. Typical types of fossil fuels include coal, petroleum (oil), and natural gas. How do Fossil Fuels contribute to Global Warming? Combustion of fossil fuels produces carbon monoxide, a toxic compound that is recognized as a greenhouse gas. Greenhouse gasses float and stay in the stratosphere of the Earth, preventing the heat on Earth from escaping into space. "Fossil Fuels." Alternative Energy, edited by Neil Schlager and Jayne Weisblatt, vol. 1: Fossil Fuels, Bioenergy, Geothermal Energy, UXL, 2006, pp. 1-55.
