  • Welcome to nouvEnergy

    A place to learn and act about Fossil Fuels, an important contributor to Global Warming

    What are Fossil Fuels?

    Substances that formed underground millions of years ago from dead plants and animals. Typical types of fossil fuels include coal, petroleum (oil), and natural gas.

    What is Global Warming?

    A term given to a recently observed rise in global temperature that is believed to be caused by humans. Many scientists believe Global Warming is caused by increasing amounts of gas in the atmosphere called greenhouse gases.

    How do Fossil Fuels contribute to Global Warming?

    Combustion of fossil fuels releases carbon monoxide, a toxic compound that is recognized as a greenhouse gas. Greenhouse gasses float and stay in the stratosphere of the Earth, preventing the heat on Earth from escaping into space.

    How can we use less of Fossil Fuels?

    Explore some of the most popular products that involves fossil fuels, and if you use any of them, consider switching to a fossil-fuel-free alternative. Also remember to spread the word!
